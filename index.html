Features

Powered by IBM Watson, Scribe provides your faculty with the tools to automate their caption and transcribing efforts. Fully open source and customizable.

  • Roadmap
  • Watch Video

User accounts

Let your faculty self-register and manage their accounts and captions.

Simple installation

Built on top of the popular Drupal CMS - as easy to install as WordPress.

Export as WebVTT

Easily export your transcription as a formatted WebVTT caption with a click of a button.

Read-along Highlighting

Visually highlights caption segments by timing to allow you to spot errors and make adjustments.

Powered by IBM Watson Speech-to-Text

The world's most powerful speech-to-text library providing you with affordable, accurate, and automated captions.

Multiple Languages Available

Easily auto-transcribe audio for multiple languages including: English, French (& French Canadian), German, Dutch, Italian, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, & Portuguese.
